History of
Uttara Adhunik Medical
College (UAMC)

Uttara Adhunik Medical College (UAMC) is the teaching and training hospital of the college. It is a 500-bedded, multidisciplinary tertiary care facility located in Uttara, Dhaka. The hospital serves patients from all over the country, particularly from Uttara, Tongi, Gazipur, and Savar.

Timeline of UAMC's Evolution

Since - 1984

Founded in 2007, UAMC, under BMSRI, is a leading medical institution affiliated with Dhaka University. With a 500-bed hospital, it excels in medical education, research, and patient care, shaping the future of healthcare in Bangladesh.

  • 1984

    The Vision Begins

    The foundation for what would become UAMC was laid with a vision for better healthcare education in Bangladesh.

  • 1986

    The First Private Medical College

    Establishment of the first private medical college in the region, setting a precedent for future developments.

  • 2003

    Foundation of Uttara Adhunik Hospital

    The hospital that would later become part of UAMC was established, focusing on providing quality healthcare services.

  • 2005

    Expansion of Indoor Services

    The hospital expanded its indoor services, increasing capacity and improving infrastructure.

  • 2007

    Birth of Uttara Adhunik Medical College (UAMC)

    UAMC was officially established under the Bangladesh Medical Science Research Institute (BMSRI).

  • 2008

    UAMC's Academic Journey Begins

    The first batch of medical students were admitted, marking the beginning of UAMC's educational mission.

  • Present

    Advancing Healthcare & Education

    UAMC continues to excel in medical education, research, and patient care, serving as a leading healthcare institution in Bangladesh.

  • Future

    Advancing Healthcare & Education

    UAMC is committed to continuing its mission of improving healthcare through education, research, and service excellence.

Uttara Adhunik Medical College (UAMC) was established in 2003 with a vision to provide quality medical education and healthcare services. Founded through the dedicated efforts of medical professionals and social leaders, UAMC is committed to training future doctors while ensuring affordable healthcare for the community.
