Services

Uttara Adhunik Medical College (UAMC) is the teaching and training hospital of the college. It is a 500-bedded, multidisciplinary tertiary care facility located in Uttara, Dhaka. The hospital serves patients from all over the country, particularly from Uttara, Tongi, Gazipur, and Savar.

Comprehensive Diagnostic Facilities

24/7 Emergency Services

Our emergency department is staffed 24/7 with experienced physicians and nurses ready to handle all types of emergencies.

  • Trauma care
  • Cardiac emergencies
  • Stroke treatment
  • Pediatric emergencies
Ambulance Services

Emergency medical transport with fully equipped ambulances and trained paramedics.

  • Basic life support
  • Advanced life support
  • Neonatal transport
  • GPS tracking for quick response
ICU (Intensive Care Unit) – 20 beds, 17 ventilators (10th floor)

State-of-the-art intensive care unit for critically ill patients requiring continuous monitoring.

  • 20 fully equipped beds
  • 17 high-capacity ventilators
  • 24/7 intensivist coverage
  • Advanced monitoring systems
CCU (Coronary Care Unit) – 17 beds (3rd floor)

Specialized unit for patients with severe cardiac conditions requiring constant care.

  • 17 cardiac monitoring beds
  • Immediate access to cardiac catheterization
  • Specialized cardiac nursing team
  • Advanced cardiac life support
NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit) – For critical neonatal cases

Specialized care for premature and critically ill newborns with advanced monitoring.

  • Level III NICU capabilities
  • Specialized neonatal ventilators
  • Neonatologists available 24/7
  • Family-centered care approach
Ambulance Services

Specialized ambulance services for various emergency situations.

  • Rapid response time
  • Trained emergency medical technicians
  • Life-saving equipment on board
  • Inter-facility transfers available
Haemodialysis Unit

Comprehensive renal replacement therapy for patients with kidney failure.

  • Modern dialysis machines
  • Specialized nephrology team
  • Regular and emergency dialysis services
  • Comfortable treatment environment
