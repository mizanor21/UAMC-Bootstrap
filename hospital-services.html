About the
Hospital

Uttara Adhunik Medical College (UAMC) is the teaching and training hospital of the college. It is a 500-bedded, multidisciplinary tertiary care facility located in Uttara, Dhaka. The hospital serves patients from all over the country, particularly from Uttara, Tongi, Gazipur, and Savar.

UAMCH Facilities & Services

MEDICAL
SERVICES

General Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of adult diseases and conditions.

Surgery

Operative procedures for treating injuries, deformities, and diseases.

Obstetrics & Gynecology

Care for women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth.

Pediatrics

Specialized medical care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Anesthesiology

Administration of anesthesia and pain management during procedures.

Radiology & Imaging

Diagnostic services including X-rays, CT scans, and ultrasounds.

Dentistry

Oral health services including diagnosis and treatment of dental disorders.

Blood Transfusion

Safe and reliable blood and component transfusion services.

CLINICAL
DEPARTMENTS

MEDICINE RELATED
Neuromedicine

Treatment of brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

Gastroenterology

Care for digestive system and gastrointestinal tract issues.

Cardiology

Diagnosis and treatment of heart and circulatory system diseases.

Nephrology

Management of kidney diseases and renal disorders.

Radiation Oncology

Cancer treatment using high-energy radiation therapy.

Psychiatry

Mental health diagnosis and therapeutic treatment.

Dermatology

Care for skin, hair, and nail disorders.

SURGICAL RELATED
Otolaryngology (ENT)

Surgery and medical treatment of ear, nose, and throat conditions.

Ophthalmology

Eye care including diagnosis and vision correction.

Neurosurgery

Surgical treatment of disorders related to the brain and spinal cord.

Urology

Surgical care for urinary tract and male reproductive organs.

Orthopedics

Treatment of bone, joint, and musculoskeletal system problems.

Emergency & Specialized Care

24/7 Emergency Services

Our emergency department is staffed 24/7 with experienced physicians and nurses ready to handle all types of emergencies.

  • Trauma care
  • Cardiac emergencies
  • Stroke treatment
  • Pediatric emergencies
Ambulance Services

Emergency medical transport with fully equipped ambulances and trained paramedics.

  • Basic life support
  • Advanced life support
  • Neonatal transport
  • GPS tracking for quick response
ICU (Intensive Care Unit) – 20 beds, 17 ventilators (10th floor)

State-of-the-art intensive care unit for critically ill patients requiring continuous monitoring.

  • 20 fully equipped beds
  • 17 high-capacity ventilators
  • 24/7 intensivist coverage
  • Advanced monitoring systems
CCU (Coronary Care Unit) – 17 beds (3rd floor)

Specialized unit for patients with severe cardiac conditions requiring constant care.

  • 17 cardiac monitoring beds
  • Immediate access to cardiac catheterization
  • Specialized cardiac nursing team
  • Advanced cardiac life support
NICU (Neonatal Intensive Care Unit) – For critical neonatal cases

Specialized care for premature and critically ill newborns with advanced monitoring.

  • Level III NICU capabilities
  • Specialized neonatal ventilators
  • Neonatologists available 24/7
  • Family-centered care approach
Ambulance Services

Specialized ambulance services for various emergency situations.

  • Rapid response time
  • Trained emergency medical technicians
  • Life-saving equipment on board
  • Inter-facility transfers available
Haemodialysis Unit

Comprehensive renal replacement therapy for patients with kidney failure.

  • Modern dialysis machines
  • Specialized nephrology team
  • Regular and emergency dialysis services
  • Comfortable treatment environment
